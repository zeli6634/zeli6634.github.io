About this app

For this app, the version of interface are 3 keys and 5 keys, and the part of body are head and hand. The instruction is that i: move up, k: move down, j: move left, l: move right and g: click the button. The 3 keys are g, j and l keys. The 5 keys are j, k, l, i and g keys. The default place for the key is at button 7. Compared with these two version, I am trying to do the same tasks: 1+2-3+4-5+6-7+8-9+0=C, 1*2/3*4/5*6/7*8/9*0=C, 7+4-1+0-8+5-2+9-6+3=C, 7*4/1*0/8*5/2*9/6*3=C and 7+5-3+8-6+9-4+2-1+0=C. All the tasks start at the same place: button 7. For 3 keys the time are: 40 seconds, 42 seconds, 43 seconds, 42 seconds and 45 seconds. For 5 keys the time are: 26 seconds, 24 seconds, 25 seconds, 24 seconds, 28 seconds. All the test I am using by hand. These is no error when I do these two tests. In conclusion, 5 keys are faster than 3 keys. Compared with head and hand, I cannot finished these tasks by head because if I use my head to click the keyboard, it will also click other keys on the keyboard. There are too many errors when I do the test. In conclusion, using different body part can highly affect the test result. The body part I am not often used is making more mistakes.
